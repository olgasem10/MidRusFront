About the project

MidRus NLP is a service for Middle Russian pos- and morphological tagging and tokenization based on UDPipe trainable pipeline. The version we are using is 1.2.1.

The current model is trained on the material of the Middle Russian subcorpus of RNC included in the collection of historical corpora. MidRus NLP is freely available for usage and testing.
